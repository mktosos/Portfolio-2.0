Personal Bio

I was born on the island of Oahu in the state of Hawaii. I graduated from Iolani School where I was a 2x highschool wrestling state and AAU national champion. I came to California to attend Cal Poly State University in San Luis Obispo to be a part of a Div 1 wrestling program, where I received an athletic scholarship. I graduated with a B.S. in Biology. I've been a professional artist, an employee and an employer, having started 2 profitable small businesses, one which still exists today. I am an avid fisherman. I love to listen to and play music. I am a regular yoga practitioner and judo blackbelt, currently enrolled in the UC Davis ext Coding Bootcamp. I live in Sacramento, CA and have a wife and two daughters.
